Biography

My name is Joshua Philipose, and I'm studying Computer Science at Georgia Tech, with a concentration in UX & AI. I'm originally from New York, but will follow opportunities wherever they are. After graduation, I'd like to be a Product Manager or SWE at a company that makes products with a toothbrush spirit - used often enough to be taken for granted, but would absolutely be missed if it disappeared.

I'm into blockchain (releasing a course soon!), predictive machine learning, and automating everything. Instead of detailing my work experience here, I'll direct you to my resume - you can download it from my home page!

Technical interests aside, I love early morning workouts, longboarding, chilling with friends, writing snappy bios for my website, and chicken parmigiana. I also enjoy the great outdoors - ALL the photos you see on this website were taken on my travels!
